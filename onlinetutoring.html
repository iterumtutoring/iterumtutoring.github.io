Why Online Tutoring?

Since the beginning of the Covid-19 Pandemic, many tutoring companies have turned to online learning to best serve their students safely. We have found that many students who were hesitant to try learning in an online environment were pleasantly surprised with how much more they prefer the convenience, flexibility and dynamic nature of online tutoring with no compromise to quality of education or student engagement!

Online, Still Local

Located in the Lower Mainland, our Tutors are intimately familiar with the BC school Curriculum. If we eventually do return to in-person learning after the pandemic, we’ll be here to serve you in the Greater Vancouver area.

Pandemic Safe!

Worried about rising case counts or the new strains? Want to take extra precautions for the safety of your family?

No need to bother with the inconveniences of masking up, sanitizing, keeping 2m distance or encasing yourself behind plexiglass. Learn worry-free from the comfort and safety of your own home!

Interactive & Engaging Technology!

We us a combination of webcam ZOOM sessions and powerful virtual whiteboard software with lots of useful features to host our tutorials. We provide ongoing technical support and train all new students on how to use the software in our first free session.

Flexible Scheduling

At iterum, we recognize that things don't always go to plan. Other tutoring companies demand as much as 3 full days advance notice to change a scheduled tutorial and will charge a full session fee for missed sessions. We work with you to schedule tutoring at a time that suits you, and ask only to be notified the day before if a session time no longer works!
